School of Computing & Design
The School of Computing & Design (SCD) educates students to apply state-of-the-art technology to solve real world communication problems in a socially responsible way. In addition to mastering the technical knowledge and skills, graduates of SCD programs excel in communication, collaboration and innovative problem solving.
